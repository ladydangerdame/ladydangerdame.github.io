Parsenip

Parsenip was a hackthon project from Developer Week 2017 in San Francisco, CA. The app was a custom slash command for Flock messenger that would use Perfecto's HAR analyzer to scan apps before deployment for web best practices. If the app needed fixes for things like broken img tags, a bot would message the Dev team through Flock, telling them what to fix!
